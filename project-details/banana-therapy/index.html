Do you feel out of place? Do you feel disconnected and uncomfortable? Do you find it hard to fit in?

To express how I feel with falling bananas animation. Tones of yellow bananas, with only one pink banana. Why must all bananas be yellow? It's okay to be different!

Matter.js, a 2D physics engine, is used to make the falling bananas animation.
